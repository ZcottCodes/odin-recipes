Ingredients

  • 500g mince meat or plant based mince
  • 2 (1.25 ounce) packages of taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • cooking spray
  • 18 6inch tortillas
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly browned. Drain, then season with taco seasoning, garlic, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. Pour in water. Simmer for 10 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place 6 tortillas into the prepared baking dish. Spread 1/3 of the salsa on top of the tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the meat mixture evenly over the salsa. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the green onions. Drop 1/2 of the sour cream randomly over the green onions. Top with 1/2 cup Cheddar and 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese. Repeat layers. Top with 6 tortillas, spread with remaining salsa, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 45 minutes or until cheeses are melted.
